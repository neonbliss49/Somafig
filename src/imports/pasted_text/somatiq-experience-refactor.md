Refine the existing Somatiq experience.

Do NOT redesign the UI.
Preserve visual style, layout, colors (light + petrol system), and components.

Change the PRODUCT LOGIC and FLOW BEHAVIOR.

---

GOAL:

Restructure the experience so that:

1. The Insight Flow becomes the PRIMARY ENTRY (morning experience)
2. The Today screen becomes a SUMMARY (post-flow home)
3. Navigation appears AFTER the flow (delayed navigation)
4. The current “Tap to understand” card is no longer the main entry point

---

PART 1 — CHANGE ENTRY LOGIC (CRITICAL)

Current behavior:
User lands on Today → taps insight → enters flow

New behavior:
User lands DIRECTLY in the Insight Flow when:

* first app open of the day
* or new significant insight is available

The flow should feel like:
→ a morning briefing
→ not something optional

---

PART 2 — REDEFINE TODAY SCREEN (SUMMARY MODE)

Transform the existing Today screen into a POST-FLOW SUMMARY screen.

This screen appears:

* after completing the flow
* on all later visits that day

---

Update Today content:

* Replace "NEW INSIGHT" card with:

State:
"Low recovery day"
"You chose: Conserve energy"

* Keep metrics section (HRV, Sleep)

* Add Plan section:
  • Reduce load (~30%)
  • Magnesium at 21:00
  • Sleep before 23:00

* Add Progress:
  • Completed / Pending actions

* Add Actions:
  [ Log how you feel ]
  [ Adjust plan ]
  [ Reopen insight ]

---

IMPORTANT:

Remove "Tap to understand" as the primary action.

Replace it with:
"Reopen insight" (secondary action)

---

PART 3 — ADD DELAYED NAVIGATION

Navigation must NOT appear during the flow.

After the final flow screen:

Add section:

"Explore further"

Options:

* See similar days (Insights)
* Understand your baseline (Body)
* View your journey (Journey)
* Log how you feel (Log)

---

PART 4 — INTRODUCE NAVIGATION AFTER TRANSITION

After user taps one of the exploration options:

Introduce a subtle bottom navigation bar:

Today | Insights | Body | Journey | +

Design:

* minimal
* low contrast
* not dominant

---

PART 5 — FLOW → SUMMARY TRANSITION

After completing the flow:

Transition back to Today (Summary screen)

Use:

* smooth fade
* slight background shift (petrol → light)

This should feel like:
→ exiting a focused mode
→ returning to daily context

---

PART 6 — KEEP PETROL SYSTEM

* Flow remains petrol-based (immersive)
* Today remains light (functional)

Do not merge them.

---

IMPORTANT:

Do NOT turn Today into a dashboard.
Do NOT reintroduce static navigation at the start.

The experience must feel like:

1. Understand (Flow)
2. Decide (Flow)
3. Operate (Today Summary)
4. Explore (Navigation)

---

The product should feel:
intentional, guided, and structured as a system.
