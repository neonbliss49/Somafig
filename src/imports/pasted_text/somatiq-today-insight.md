Design a premium mobile app screen for a personal health intelligence product called “Somatiq”.

This is the “Today” screen.

IMPORTANT:
This is NOT a dashboard.
This screen is built around ONE core moment:
A personal pattern discovery that explains the user’s current state.

---

CORE EXPERIENCE GOAL

The first thing the user sees must feel like:

“I didn’t know this about my body — but it explains exactly how I feel.”

The UI must be centered around a single insight, not multiple equal sections.

---

STRUCTURE (STRICT HIERARCHY)

1. HERO MOMENT (dominant, top of screen, large typography)

Display a bold, emotionally impactful insight:

“Every time your energy dropped like this,
your body showed this exact pattern first.”

Below it:

“Today is part of that pattern.”

This is the most important element on the screen.
It should take up significant visual space and attention.

---

2. EVIDENCE (directly below, visually connected)

Show WHY this statement is true:

* “Seen in 4 of your last 6 similar days”

Add a small visual pattern indicator (e.g. dots or mini timeline):
• ● ● ● ○ ● ○
Highlight today

Keep it minimal but clearly visible.

---

3. DRIVERS (CAUSES)

Explain what is driving this pattern:

Title: “What’s driving this today”

* 3 short nights
* HRV below your baseline
* Temperature +0.4°C
* Luteal phase (Day 18)

Make this feel like supporting evidence, not a separate card.

---

4. MEANING (EMOTIONAL INTERPRETATION)

Short, human explanation:

“You are not underperforming.
Your system is reallocating energy.”

This should feel reassuring and personal.

---

5. ACTION (CLEAR DECISION)

Title: “What matters today”

Show a structured plan:

NOW
Take a short walk to reset

TODAY
Reduce cognitive load by ~30%

TONIGHT
Take magnesium at 21:00

PLAN
Sleep before 23:00

Primary button:
“Mark as done”

This section should feel like a clear plan, not a list.

---

6. CONSEQUENCE / PREDICTION

Title: “What happens if you push today”

“If you push through today,
tomorrow’s energy drop will likely be stronger.”

Then:

“What’s likely next”

* Energy dip continues tomorrow
* Recovery peak expected Monday

---

IMPORTANT DESIGN RULES

* Do NOT create many equal-sized cards
* Do NOT make this look like Apple Health or Whoop
* The layout must feel like a vertical story, not modules
* The hero insight must dominate the screen
* Everything else supports that insight

---

VISUAL STYLE

* Calm, premium, minimal
* Soft neutral background
* Muted accent color (teal / blue)
* Large, readable typography
* Generous spacing
* Subtle separators (not heavy cards)

---

TONE

* Intelligent
* Calm
* Slightly surprising
* Reassuring
* Never alarmist

---

FINAL CHECK

When looking at the screen, it should feel like:

“This app understands patterns in my body better than I do.”

NOT:

“This app shows my health data.”
