Design a premium mobile app screen for a personal health intelligence product.

This is the “Today” screen.

IMPORTANT:
This screen does NOT explain insights again.
The insight already happened in the check-in.

This screen represents the user’s CURRENT STATE and guides their day.

---

STRUCTURE

1. STATE HEADER (small, not dominant)

Display the chosen mode:

“Conserving energy”

Supporting line:

“Low recovery day”

Keep it compact and calm. No large hero text.

---

2. CURRENT STATUS (primary focus)

Show one clear state:

Examples:

* Pattern active
* Holding steady
* Slightly stabilizing
* Pushing beyond capacity

This is the most important element.

No numbers, no charts.

---

3. REALITY RE-CHECK (subtle interaction)

Question:

“Still aligned with your current state?”

Options:

* Yes
* Not really

One-tap interaction.
Optional, not forced.

---

4. ADAPTIVE RESPONSE (changes based on input)

If aligned:
“You’re supporting recovery well so far”

If not aligned:
“You’re pushing beyond your recovery capacity”

Keep it short and human.

---

5. TODAY FOCUS (max 3 actions)

Structure:

NOW
→ one action

NEXT
→ one action

TONIGHT
→ one action

No long lists. No checkboxes overload.

---

6. PROGRESSION (lightweight)

Optional:

* “1 of 3 completed”
* “Walk completed”

No gamification pressure.

---

7. LOOK AHEAD (short)

1–2 lines only:

“Energy dip likely continues tomorrow”
“Recovery peak expected Monday”

---

8. OPTIONAL LINKS

* See similar days
* Understand baseline
* View your journey

Low visual priority.

---

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

* Calm, minimal, premium
* No dashboard feeling
* No repeated explanation from check-in
* One clear state, not multiple competing sections
* Feels like a companion, not a data tool

---

FINAL FEELING

“I know where I stand right now and what to do next.”
