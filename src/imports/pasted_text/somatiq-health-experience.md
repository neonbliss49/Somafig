Design a premium iOS mobile app experience for a health intelligence product called "Somatiq".

This is NOT a static app screen.
This is a guided, interactive experience that unfolds step by step.

---

GOAL:

Create a complete “Today” experience that:

* reveals insight progressively
* lets the user understand cause and effect
* asks the user to make a decision
* shows consequences
* offers deeper exploration (without a navbar)

The experience should feel like:
→ a conversation with your body
→ not a dashboard

---

TARGET USER:

Women 35–55 experiencing fatigue, hormonal changes, and unpredictable energy.

Tone:
Calm, intelligent, emotionally reassuring, premium.

---

CREATE A MULTI-STEP FLOW (5 CONNECTED SCREENS)

---

### SCREEN 1 — HERO (ENTRY STATE)

Content:

* Greeting:
  "Good Morning, Hanna"

* Main insight (large, dominant typography):
  "Your body is entering a low-recovery phase today"

* Subtle CTA:
  "See what’s driving this ↓"

Design:

* Minimal, lots of whitespace
* Strong typography hierarchy
* Calm background

---

### SCREEN 2 — CAUSALITY (REVEAL)

Content:

* Same insight (slightly reduced size)

* Visual cause chain (CRITICAL):

Sleep depth reduced (3 short nights)
→ HRV dropped 15%
→ Reduced recovery capacity

* Luteal phase (Day 18)

- Pattern validation:
  "Seen in 4 of your last 6 similar cycles"

- Personal framing:
  "This pattern matches your recent cycle behavior"

Design:

* Use vertical or connected flow (not bullet points)
* Subtle lines or connectors between elements
* Feels structured and intelligent

---

### SCREEN 3 — INTERPRETATION (MEANING)

Content:

"This isn’t random.

Your body is shifting energy
from performance toward regulation."

* Add emotional reassurance:
  "You’re not underperforming — your system is adapting."

Design:

* Minimal, calm
* Give this moment visual breathing space

---

### SCREEN 4 — DECISION (CORE INTERACTION)

Content:

Question:
"How do you want to approach today?"

Two options:

[ Conserve energy ]
"Stabilize faster, reduce fatigue tomorrow"

[ Push through ]
"Short-term output, higher fatigue risk tomorrow"

Design:

* Clear contrast between options
* Feels like a real choice with consequences

---

### SCREEN 5 — RESPONSE + OUTCOME + NAVIGATION

If user selects "Conserve energy":

* Confirmation:
  "Conserving energy today"

* Plan:

• Reduce cognitive & physical load by ~30%
• Take 400mg Magnesium at 21:00
• Aim for sleep before 23:00

* Proof (integrated):
  "This approach improved your deep sleep in 3 of your last 7 similar patterns (+25 min)"

* Outcome framing:
  "This likely prevents a deeper energy drop tomorrow"

* Future:
  "Energy dip likely continues tomorrow. Recovery peak expected Monday."

---

### CONTEXTUAL NAVIGATION (CRITICAL)

At the bottom, add:

"Explore further"

[ See similar days ]
[ Understand your HRV baseline ]
[ Ask Somatiq ]

These should feel like natural extensions, not a menu.

---

VISUAL STYLE:

* Premium, calm, minimal
* Soft gradients, subtle shadows
* No dashboards, no clutter
* Strong typography hierarchy
* Avoid too many cards

---

PROTOTYPE INSTRUCTIONS:

* Screen 1 → Screen 2 (tap CTA)
* Screen 2 → Screen 3 (auto or tap)
* Screen 3 → Screen 4 (tap)
* Screen 4 → Screen 5 (based on selection)

Use Smart Animate (300–400ms, ease-out)

---

IMPORTANT:

Do NOT design this as a typical app.

Design it as:
→ a guided experience
→ a system that reveals understanding step by step

The result should feel:
intelligent, personal, and deeply trustworthy.
